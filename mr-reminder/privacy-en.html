Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 1, 2026

Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how Mr.Reminder (the "App") handles personal information. By using the App, you agree to this policy.

Contact

  • Email: yu.development.vtoz+reminder@gmail.com

Information We Collect

Information Stored by the App

The App stores the following information locally on your device. This data is never transmitted to our servers.

Type of Information Purpose Storage Location
Reminder data (title, notes, date/time, repeat settings)Reminder management and notificationsDevice only
Settings (theme, language, sort order)App customizationDevice only
Notification schedulesPush notification deliveryDevice only

Information We Do Not Collect

The App does not collect the following information:

  • Personally identifiable information such as name, address, or phone number
  • Location data
  • Contact information
  • Usage data from other apps

Communication with External Services

Advertising (Google AdMob)

The free version of the App displays advertisements using Google AdMob (Google LLC).

Data collected:

  • Advertising identifier (IDFA / GAID) — only when permitted via ATT
  • Ad interaction data (impressions, clicks, etc.)
  • Device information (model, OS, screen size)
  • Approximate location (IP address-based)

AdMob may use the advertising identifier for personalized ad delivery. On iOS, the advertising identifier is only used for ad delivery when the user grants permission through the App Tracking Transparency (ATT) dialog. If permission is denied, non-personalized ads are displayed.

When subscribed to the Premium plan, ads are hidden and no data is sent to AdMob.

Details: Google Privacy Policy

Subscription Management (RevenueCat)

The App uses RevenueCat (RevenueCat, Inc.) for subscription management.

Data collected:

  • Purchase history (product ID, purchase date, expiration date)
  • Subscription status (active/cancelled/expired)
  • Anonymous app user ID

Data not collected:

  • Credit card numbers or bank account information (payments are processed by App Store / Google Play)
  • Personally identifiable information such as name or address

Details: RevenueCat Privacy Policy

Crash Reporting (Sentry)

The App sends crash and error information to Sentry (Functional Software, Inc.) to improve app stability.

Data sent:

  • Error type and stack trace (program execution path)
  • Device information (OS type, OS version, device model)
  • App version information
  • Screen navigation history (to identify which screen the error occurred on)

Data not sent:

  • Reminder content or notes
  • Personally identifiable information

Crash reporting is only active in production builds and is not sent during development.

Details: Sentry Privacy Policy

Payment Processing

Subscription payments are processed through the App Store or Google Play. Personal payment information (such as credit card numbers) is handled according to each platform's privacy policy. The Operator does not have access to this information.

App Tracking Transparency (ATT)

On iOS, the App requests tracking permission through the ATT dialog for ad delivery purposes.

  • If permitted: The advertising identifier (IDFA) is used for personalized ad delivery
  • If denied: Non-personalized ads are displayed. App functionality is not affected

You can change ATT settings at any time in Settings > Privacy & Security > Tracking on your device.

Data Storage and Management

Local Storage

Reminder data and settings are stored exclusively on your device using encrypted storage (MMKV encrypted storage).

Automatic Backup

The App maintains automatic backups (up to 3 generations) on your device. Backup data is never transmitted externally.

Data Deletion

You can delete your data in the following ways:

  1. Delete individually within the App: Delete reminders one by one
  2. Delete all data within the App: Delete all data from the Settings screen
  3. Uninstall the App: All data is deleted

Third-Party Disclosure

The App does not sell, share, or provide your information to any third parties other than the external services described above (AdMob, RevenueCat, Sentry).

Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for children under 13 years of age, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Security

The App implements the following security measures:

  • Reminder data is stored encrypted on the device and is never transmitted externally
  • Data is protected by device security features (passcode, biometric authentication, etc.)
  • Data sent externally (crash reports, etc.) does not contain personally identifiable information

Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated as needed. Significant changes will be communicated through the App. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Governing Law

This policy shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of Japan.
