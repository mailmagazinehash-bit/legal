Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 4, 2026

1. Introduction

Mr.Reminder ("the App") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information in the App.

2. Operator Information

  • Operator: Yuki Oga
  • Contact: yu.development.vtoz@gmail.com

3. Information We Collect

The App stores reminder data (title, message, notification time, repeat settings, etc.) only on your device. This data is never sent to external servers.

When using the contact feature, device information (OS, device model, app version, etc.) is included in the email.

4. Advertising

The App uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect your device advertising ID and approximate location for ad delivery. Please refer to Google's Privacy Policy for details.

5. Error Logging

The App uses Sentry to collect error information to improve service quality. The information collected includes:

  • Device information (OS, model, app version)
  • Stack trace when an error occurs
  • Screen information when an error occurs

This information is anonymized and cannot be used to identify individuals. The collected information is used only to improve the App.

6. Notifications

The App uses push notifications for reminder functionality. You can change notification permissions at any time in your device settings.

7. Data Deletion

Uninstalling the App will delete all reminder data stored on your device.

8. Information Sharing

The App does not provide, sell, or share your information with third parties (except for advertising and error logging purposes).

9. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

10. Changes to Privacy Policy

This Policy may be changed as necessary. If there are significant changes, we will notify you within the App.

11. Contact

For questions about this Privacy Policy, please use the contact feature in the App or email us at:

  • Email: yu.development.vtoz@gmail.com
