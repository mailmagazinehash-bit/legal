Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how Tabilog (the "App") handles your personal information. By using this App, you agree to the terms of this Policy.

Information We Collect

Information Collected by the App

The App stores the following information locally on your device. This information is NOT transmitted to external servers (except where noted).

Type of Information Purpose Storage Location
Trip records (name, dates, destinations) Trip management and display Device only
Event information (title, notes, time) Recording trip events Device only
Photo files and thumbnails Photo display in trip records Device only
Photo EXIF data (date/time, GPS coordinates) Auto-filling event date/time and location Device only (extracted and used locally)
Album data (layouts, text) Custom album creation Device only
App settings (theme, display style) App personalization Device only
Purchase information Subscription management App Store / Google Play (external)

Information We Do NOT Collect

The App does NOT collect:

  • Personally identifiable information such as name, address, or phone number
  • Contact information
  • Usage of other apps
  • Advertising identifiers

Communication with External Services

Address Search (Geocoding)

When searching for or registering trip destinations, the App sends queries to the following external services:

  • Nominatim (OpenStreetMap): Address search and reverse geocoding
  • Photon (Komoot): Supplementary address search

Only search keywords or coordinates are transmitted to these services. No user-identifying information (account details, device identifiers, etc.) is sent.

Payment Processing

Subscription purchases and management are processed through the App Store or Google Play. Personal information related to billing is handled according to each platform's privacy policy.

Photos and EXIF Data

The App imports only photos explicitly selected by the user. During import, EXIF data (date/time, GPS coordinates) is read to assist with event date/time and location input.

Extracted EXIF data is used only on the device and is NOT transmitted to external servers.

Data Storage and Management

Local Storage

All App data (except billing information) is stored exclusively on your device.

Data Deletion

You can delete your data by:

  1. Deleting within the App: Trips and events can be deleted individually. Associated photo files are also deleted
  2. Uninstalling the App: All data will be deleted
  3. Clearing app data from device settings: All data will be deleted

Third-Party Sharing

The App does not provide, sell, or share your information with third parties.

Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Security

The App implements the following security measures:

  • Data is stored only on your device and is not transmitted externally
  • Data is protected by your device's security features (passcode, biometric authentication, etc.)

Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Policy may be updated as necessary. Significant changes will be communicated through the App. Your continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated Policy.
